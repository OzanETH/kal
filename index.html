Serological
Tests

In serologic tests Antibodies are diverse. So, there are various tests for detecting the presence of different types of antibodies. These include: An agglutination assay shows whether antibodies exposed to certain antigens will cause particle clumping. A precipitation test shows whether the antigens are similar by measuring the presence of antibodies in body fluids. The Western blot test identifies the presence of antimicrobial antibodies in your blood by their reaction with target antigens.
